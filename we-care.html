Temporary shelter of dogs "Chance"

Children with developmental delays live in the Polivanovsky orphanage and the Verkhnedneprovsky orphanage # 2. The children of these orphanages are from 6 to 30 years old (development is the same as in children 4-12 years old). September for them is the same beginning of the new academic year as for all schoolchildren.

Children's opportunities are limited. Thanks to special programs, children learn about the world through games, handicrafts and creativity. As far as the physical, mental and mental development of pupils allows, they acquire skills that will help them adapt to life in society.

For two weeks of our charitable campaign to raise funds to orphanages for the Day of Knowledge, 13,500 UAH were collected. With this money we purchased a huge amount of stationery and kits for creativity.

Due to the quarantine, assistance was sent by mail, for security reasons.
